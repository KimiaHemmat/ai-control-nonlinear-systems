- # Classical

- **Control Lyapunov & Barrier Functions (CLF/CBF):** [IEEE Paper](https://www.ieee-jas.net/en/article/doi/10.1109/JAS.2023.123075)
  - Summary: Presents a unified framework for ensuring stability (Lyapunov) and safety (Barrier functions) in nonlinear systems using optimization-based control.

- **Event-Based and Quantized Nonlinear Control:** [Springer Article](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11633-015-0906-x)
  - Summary: Introduces event-triggered strategies that reduce computation by updating control only when needed while maintaining stability.

- **Event-Triggered Control of Nonlinear Stochastic Systems:** [DOI Link](https://doi.org/10.1109/tac.2019.2916285)
  - Summary: Addresses nonlinear systems with uncertainty, ensuring stability under stochastic disturbances with reduced control updates.

- **Nonlinear Model Predictive Control (MPC):** [DOI Link](https://doi.org/10.1109/tac.2018.2800789)
  - Summary: Optimization-based control for nonlinear systems that balances performance, constraints, and stability.
